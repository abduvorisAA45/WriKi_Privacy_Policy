# Privacy Policy — WriKi

**Last updated:** June 7, 2025  
**Developer:** Team WriKi  
**Contact:** wriki5555@gmail.com

---

## 1. Overview

WriKi is an educational platform for teachers and students. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

By using WriKi, you agree to the terms described in this document.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data you provide directly

| Data | Required | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| Email address | Yes | Account creation and authentication |
| Display name / nickname | No | Identifying yourself in the app (real name not required) |
| Files and documents | No | Submitting assignment answers; sharing learning materials |
| Comments and messages | No | Discussing assignments with teachers or students |
| Photos (as .png files) | No | Submitting handwritten work as images |

### 2.2 Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect:

- Real name (you may use any nickname)
- Phone number
- Physical address
- Location data
- Browsing history
- Financial information
- Health information
- Audio recordings
- Contact lists from your device

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to provide the WriKi service:

- **Email** — account authentication and password reset via one-time code (OTP).
- **Files and comments** — displaying assignment submissions, grades, and discussions within the platform.
- **Photos** — storing and displaying image files uploaded as assignment answers.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on our own server (Hetzner, Germany).
- Uploaded files (documents, images) are stored in Cloudflare R2 — a secure cloud storage service.
- Passwords are stored in hashed form and are never stored in plain text.
- All data is transmitted over an encrypted connection (HTTPS / TLS).
- We do not share your data with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

WriKi does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

No user data is transferred to third parties.

---

## 6. Your Rights

### Account and data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by:

- Using the **"Send a feedback"** option in your profile within the app, or
- Emailing us at **wriki5555@gmail.com**

We will process your request within a reasonable time.

### Data related to groups

When a teacher removes a student from a group, that student's access to the group's content is automatically revoked.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

WriKi is intended for users aged **13 and older**.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at wriki5555@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the **"Last updated"** date at the top of this document.

We recommend checking this page periodically.
